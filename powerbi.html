Power Bi

Power BI offers a wide range of visualization options, including charts, graphs, maps, and tables.

Allows users to connect to a variety of data sources, transform and shape data, and create data models.

Below I worked with real problems, practicing visualization techniques, and improving with interactive hands-on projects.

Olympic History

Olympic history data allows a compelling and insightful exploration of the Games' rich legacy. Through interactive dashboards and reports, we can uncover patterns, trends, and historical achievements, offering a data-driven perspective on the evolution of this global sporting event. From medal counts to host cities, and to engage with this data in a visually engaging and informative way. Whether for research, analysis, or simply celebrating the Olympic spirit.

Road Accidents UK 2021-2022

Analysis of road accidents is crucial to understand the factors involved and their impact. Accidents usually involve multiple variables like time, weather conditions, age of driver, etc. and hence it is challenging to analyze the data.

Human Resources Attendance

The Power BI Dashboard for HR Attendance Analytics is a robust data visualization solution designed to assist companies in monitoring their employees' attendance. This dashboard offers a streamlined presentation of essential attendance metrics, including absenteeism, trends in remote work, sick leave percentages, and more. Through Power BI's interactive visualizations, users can promptly recognize attendance data trends, patterns, and irregularities, empowering them to make well-informed decisions and implement actions to enhance attendance management.

Sales Analysis

Power BI is a powerful tool for sales analysis, allowing gain valuable insights from sales data. Identify key sales metrics to track, such as total revenue, sales growth, profit margins, and customer acquisition rates.Analyze sales data over time to spot trends and seasonality. Create time-based visualizations to understand how sales performance changes over weeks, months, or years.

Hospitality Data

AtliQ Grands owns multiple five-star hotels across India. They have been in the hospitality industry for the past 20 years. Due to strategic moves from other competitors and ineffective decision-making in management, AtliQ Grands are losing its market share and revenue in the luxury/business hotels category. As a strategic move, the managing director of AtliQ Grands wanted to incorporate “Business and Data Intelligence” to regain their market share and revenue. However, they do not have an in-house data analytics team to provide them with these insights.
